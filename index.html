Welcome to MINIMA!

Minima is a chrome extension which helps in increasing the efficiency of the non-technical users(like PMs, Designers ...) to quickly change, experiment, and play around with styling, content of the existing website without the need to code and either publish them or share with others for feedback.

How to Install Minima?

Minima is a chrome extension, hence it needs to be installed from chrome webstore. To quickly install it follow the link down below and then click on "add to extension" there, when Minima icon appears at the menu bar loacted at top-right of your browser, that means Minima has been installed and you can come back here on our Webpage.
